Who We Are

We're a team of scientists, innovators, and collaborators dedicated to advancing the frontiers of immunology. With expertise spanning research, diagnostics, and technology, we work at the intersection of science and impact.

Driven by discovery and united by purpose, we transform complex immunological insights into practical, real-world solutions that improve health outcomes on a global scale. Every breakthrough we pursue is rooted in a deep commitment to collaboration, integrity, and the future of human health.

Trusted by Industry Leaders

Trusted by hospitals, pharmacies, and government health institutions, we deliver diagnostics that care at every level of the healthcare system.

Purpose-Driven Science

We aim to transform cutting-edge immunology into practical, scalable solutions that address real-world health challenges and improve outcomes for patients, providers, and public health systems alike.

The Future We're Building

We see a future where accurate, rapid diagnostics are not just accessible—but foundational to healthcare decisions everywhere, empowering clinicians and communities across every corner of the world.

What We Stand For

We're committed to doing meaningful work with honesty, precision, and a shared responsibility to improve global health. Our values shape how we conduct research, build partnerships, and bring innovative diagnostics to the communities that need them most.
